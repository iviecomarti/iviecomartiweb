About Me

I’m a Biotechnologist with a Master’s in Neuroscience, currently pursuing a PhD focused on artificial 3D tumor models of neuroblastoma. I specialize in digital pathology, and AI for biomedical image analysis. FPU scholarship

AI & Digital Pathology Projects

DANEELpath

DANEELpath is a QuPath toolkit designed to streamline the segmentation and spatial feature obtention from artificial 3D tumors of Neuroblastoma. Those 3D tumors are used to deep into tumor biology and drug testing. More DANEELpath info:

Overview Video Preprint GitHub

OvoPath

OvoPath is an open-source tool for analyzing ovarian follicles in histological images on QuPath. It offers a streamlined, reproducible pipeline for follicle classification across stages, supporting standardized, high-throughput analysis in reproductive biology. More info:

Paper GitHub

GISKARDscope

GISKARDscope is a prototype system that uses deep learning to analyze microscope images in real time. It’s designed to support pathologists by enhancing diagnostic speed and accuracy. Want to learn more? Let’s talk!
